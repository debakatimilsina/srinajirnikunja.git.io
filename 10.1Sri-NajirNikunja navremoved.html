Netrabandhu Poudel v. Dr. Govinda K.C (Facts of the Case):Go To Contents

The Supreme Court ordered to reinstate Dr. Shashi Sharma as a Dean of Institute of Medicine, Maharajganj. This very court order embarrassed Dr. Govinda K.C. with rage and anger. Dr. Govinda K.C started a hunger strike demanding the resignation of Chief Justice Gopal Parajuli accusing him corrupted and mafia. Supreme Court Officer Netrabandhu Paudyal submitted a report demanding punishment for Dr. Govinda K.C. in the contempt of court for such disappointing act. According to the Supreme Court's order in the very report, Govind K.C. was arrested and was presented to the court at 9 o'clock the next morning by the police.

Toyanath Dhungana Vs. Kantipur Publication Pvt. Ltd. Also the facts of the case:
Toyanath Dhungana filed the Contempt of Court petition in the Supreme Court to punish the media including Kantipur Dainik for creating confusion in the public mind, undermining the faith in the judiciary and obstructing the administration of justice by publishing misleading news about Chief Justice Gopal Parajuli under the title of 4 types of birth dates of the Chief Justice. On the petition, the court issued summons to the respondents to appear before the court to testify.
Statement of Govinda K.C
Speaking of wrong decisions and acts of judges is not contempt but respect. A healthy criticism of a judgment does not constitute contempt of court. My protest is against the wrong judge, not against the judiciary. If Chief Justice Gopal Parajuli is brought under judicial investigation and action is taken upon him, the judiciary will become the center of faith.I have not committed any contempt of court.
Written statement and pleading note of Kantipur Daily et.al.
We do not intend to harm the dignity of the court. Our aim is to increase faith and trust in the court. Gopal Parajuli's citizenship and educational certificate are not created by us. Publication of what appears in the government records does not constitute contempt of court. Let the petition for contempt of court be dismissed.
Verdict of Supreme Court:
The news published by Kantipur Dainik et.al did not confirm the contempt of the court.Eventhough Govind K.C's expression and statement were indecent, the principle of fair hearing was not properly followed in the proceeding so court cannot convict him. Thus, both the claims would be quashed.

RATIO & PRECEDENTS:
  • It is better to ignore than to care: A judge sometimes has to listen to negative feedback and comments. Pursuing them all is not possible. If such comments do not adversely affect the administration of justice, it is better for them to ignore them.

  • Tolerant democracy is today's requisite: It is necessary today to strengthen the concept of tolerant democracy by maintaining a proper balance between freedom of expression and the power of court to punish in its contempt.

  • Indiscriminate use of weapon of contempt will wound the justice: Justice should not be perverted by indiscriminate use of the weapon of contempt of court.

  • The main responsibilities assigned to the Supreme Court by the Constitution:
    (a) to exercise the judicial power
    (b) to retain intact its judicial authority as a court of record and that of subordinate courts
    (c) to punish in the contempt if anyone obstructs the administration of justice or disobeys an order or judgment.

  • The following actions fall within the scope of contempt and the court has the right to stop such actions and punish the guilty:
    (a) If any publication or expression obstructs the course of justice
    (b) if anyone seeks to create confusion in judicial proceedings by causing undue defamation
    (c) if anyone acts with the intention of deviating the the court, its judges or employees, litigants and legal practitioners from the course of justice
    (d) if anyone acts with ill intention to degrade the reputation of the Judiciary in the eyes of the public

  • Courts should also respect matters guaranteed as the rights of individuals:
    Respecting the right to a fair hearing is the primary responsibility of the court. It has to be followed although no one demands it. Courts should also respect the subjects guaranteed by the constitution as the rights of individuals.

  • संवैधानिक इजलासको सहज सञ्चालन मौलिक हकको विषय हुनेः व्यावहारिकताको नाउँमा संविधान, कानून एवं न्यायका मान्य सिद्धान्त प्रतिकूलका काम कारबाही स्वीकार्य हुँदैनन्। तर संविधान, कानून एवं न्यायका मान्य सिद्धान्तअनुकूल हुने अवस्थामा न्यायिक काम कारबाहीलाई व्यवहार-सुलभ र सहज तुल्याउन सकिने। संवैधानिक इजलासको व्यवस्था नागरिकको मौलिक हकको प्रचलनसँग जोडिएको विषय हो। यसको अभावमा मौलिक हकको प्रचलन सम्भव हुँदैन। निरन्तर र अविच्छिन्न रूपमा संवैधानिक इजलास कायम हुनुपर्ने र क्रियाशील रहनुपर्ने कुरा स्वयंमा मौलिक हकको विषय हुने।

  • न्यायिक अभ्यासलाई व्यवहारिक बनाउनुपर्नेः संवैधानिक इजलास शून्य रहन नसक्नेः संवैधानिक प्रावधानहरूको कार्यान्वयनका लागि कहिल्यै कुनै अवस्थामा पनि संवैधानिक इजलास अपूर्ण रहन नहुने। नेपालको संविधानले संवैधानिक इजलासको शून्यताको स्थितिको परिकल्पना गरेको नदेखिने।

SUMMARY
Constitution of Neal provides for a constitutional bench in the Supreme Court. The Judicial Council has to recommend 4 justices for that session. Although 10 months has elapsed without full members in the Constitutional Bench; the Judicial Council has not recommended justices for it. Due to such inaction of the Judicial Council, the issues to be considered from that session have piled up. Issues of complex and serious nature have also come to a standstill. A writ petition has been filed in the Supreme Court against the Judicial Council and its President for not showing any readiness to fulfill their constitutional duty. An order has been issued by the Supreme Court in the name of the Judicial Council to expedite the recommendation of a sufficient number of judges for the Constitutional Court as soon as possible (within 15 days).

VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
Issuance of Mandamus Order: An order was issued in the name of the Judicial Council to recommend a sufficient number of judges for the constitutional Bench of the Supreme Court as soon as possible (within 15 days) .
RATIO & PRECEDENTS
  • Smooth operation of Constitution Bench is a Fundamental Right: The provision of constitutional bench is related to the exercise of fundamental rights of citizens. Without it, the exercise of fundamental rights is not possible. The fact that the constitutional bench should be maintained continuously and uninterruptedly and be active is a matter of fundamental right in itself. 

  • Constitution never envisages a vacant Constitutional Bench: For the implementation of constitutional provisions, Constitutional Benchshould never be incomplete.Constitution does not envisage a vacuum of constitutional Bench.

  • Panel of Judges is better to the precise number of judges: Considering the recognition and practicality of judicial performance, a panel of judges can be formed by recommending the names of more than four judges for the constitutional bench.

  • Judicial Work must be made practicable as far as possible: In the name of practicality, actions contrary to the accepted principles of constitution, law and justice are not acceptable. However, in accordance with the constitution, law and the accepted principles of justice, judicial work can be made practical and easy.

वातावरण प्रभाव मुल्याङ्कन (EIA) नै नगरी पर्सा जिल्लाको ठोरीदेखि नवलपरासीको त्रिवेणी जोड्ने हुलाकीमार्ग (करिव ३० कि.मी) चितवन राष्ट्रिय निकुञ्जभित्रबाट बनाउन लागे। अन्तर्राष्ट्रिय महासन्धिहरूसमेतको विपरीत हुने गरी कुनै सडक बन्‍नु हुँदैन र निकुञ्‍जको सम्पदा नोक्सान हुने गरी गरिन लागेका कार्यहरू रोकी पाउँ भनी सर्वोच्च अदालतमा सार्वजनिक सरोकारयुक्त रिट निवेदन दायर भयो। अदालतमा उजुरी परेको सुइको पाएपछि सरकारले हतार हतार एउटा टोलि गठन गरी गोलमोटल ढङ्गले वातावरणीय प्रभावको मूल्यांकन (EIA) गरायो। सो टोलिले बुझाएको वातावरणीय प्रभाव मूल्याङ्कन अध्ययन प्रतिवेदन नेपाल सरकारबट स्वीकृत भई रिट निवेदनको अन्तिम सुनुवाई हुनु अगावै अदालतसमक्ष पेश पनि भयो। झारा टारूवा किसिमले प्रक्रियामात्र पूरा गर्ने उद्देश्यले वातावरणीय प्रभावको अध्ययन (EIA) भएको भन्दै उक्त प्रतिवेदनलाई अदालतले अस्वीकार गर्‍यो। त्यस्तो हास्यास्पद र लज्जास्पद अध्ययन प्रतिवेदनलाई स्वीकृती प्रदान गर्ने गरी नेपाल सरकारबाट भएको निर्णयलाई उत्प्रेषणको आदेशले बदर पनि गर्‍यो। चितवन राष्ट्रिय निकुञ्‍जको कार्यालय एवं युनेस्कोलगायतका सरोकारवाला निकायहरूको परामर्श र सहमतिमा गरिएको वातावरणीय प्रभाव अध्ययनले अनुमति नदिएसम्म चितवन राष्ट्रिय निकुञ्‍जभित्र पर्ने गरी सडक निर्माणसम्बन्धी कुनै कार्य नगर्नु नगराउनु भनी परमादेश पनि जारी भयो।
सर्वोच्च अदालतको आदेश(फैसला)

  • चितवन राष्ट्रिय निकुञ्‍ज कार्यालय एवं युनेस्कोलगायतका सरोकारवाला निकायहरूको परामर्श र सहमतिमा गरिएको वातावरणीय प्रभाव अध्ययनले अनुमति नदिएसम्म चितवन राष्ट्रिय निकुञ्‍जभित्र पर्ने गरी सडक निर्माणसम्बन्धी कुनै कार्य नगर्नु नगराउनु भनी परमादेश पनि जारी गर्‍यो।
निर्णयाधार र प्रतिपादित सिद्धान्त
  • सार्वजनिक सरोकारको निवेदन भनेको आवाज विहिनहरूको आवाज होः आवाज हराएका एवं आफ्नो कुरा आफैँ भन्‍न नसक्ने व्यक्ति र समुदायको तर्फबाट पेस गरिएको निवेदन वा अदालतलाई दिइएको सूचना नै सार्वजनिक सरोकारको निवेदन हो। वातावरणीय न्यायको सन्दर्भमा सार्वजनिक सरोकारको निवेदनलाई सम्पूर्ण प्राणी चराचरको तर्फबाट गरिएको याचनाको रुपमा बुझ्नुपर्ने

SUMMARY
The postal road (about 30 km) connecting Thori of Parsa to Triveni of Nawalparasi district is being constructed through Chitwan National Park without conducting Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA). A public interest writ petition has been filed in the Supreme Court seeking that no road should be built contrary to international conventions and that works being done to damage the park's assets should be stopped. After knowing about the complaint in the court, the government hastily formed a team and conducted a round-the-clock environmental impact assessment (EIA). The environmental impact assessment study report submitted by the team was approved by the government and submitted to the court before the final hearing of the writ petition. The court rejected the report, saying that the environmental impact assessment (EIA) has been prepared in weedy way aiming to fulfill the formality. The government decision to approve such a ridiculous and shameful study report has also been overturned by the order of Certiorari. A Mandamus order has also been issued not to carry out any road construction work within the Chitwan National Park unless the Environmental Impact Study, in consultation and agreement with the concerned bodies including the Chitwan National Park Office and UNESCO, gives permission.
VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
  • Rejection of EIA Report:

    The EIA report was rejected by the court because it was hurriedly prepared without thorough study.

    The court rejected the EIA report stating that the report has encouraged the uncontrolled exploitation and fragmentation of natural resources. Moreover, in order to give formal approval to road construction, the report had misused and ridiculed the scientific methods of assessment and did not explicitly review adequate alternatives. The court overturned the decision of the Ministry of Forests and Environment to accept such bogus EIA report.

  • Issuance of writ of Mandamus: A mandamus order was also issued not to carry out any work related to the construction of roads within the Chitwan National Park unless the permission is granted by environmental impact study conducted in consultation and in agreement with the concernedbodies including the Chitwan National Parks Office and UNESCO.

RATIO-DECIDENDI AND ESTABLISHED PRECEDENTS
  • Public Interest Litigation (PIL) is voice of voiceless: A petition filed on behalf of a person and the community who has lost their voice and cannot raise their voice for themselves or the information given to the court is a petition of public interest. In the context of environmental justice, the petition of public concern should be understood as a petition made on behalf of all living beings.

  • Government is the trustee of Public property: The government is only a trustee and not the owner of public property. Therefore, the government has no authority to use natural resources only for the benefit of a limited number of individuals or communities.

  • The Consent must be obtained from the very authority to whom the law demands so: Permission must be obtained from the person from whom the law demands. The consent obtained from the higher bodies will not fulfill the legal requirement if law specifies some other bodies for obtaining the consent.

  • Road construction is not panacea for solving all problems: Even if the demands for access to education, health and employment of the people of the settlements within the park and the people of Thori on the southern border are justified, the only solution cannot be road construction and expansion.

  • Government must not act whimsically: It is not appropriate for the government to express its commitment to the world community to abide by conventions but act whimsically within the country.

  • Protector should not be the Destroyer: It is not advisable for state-run bodies to be arrogant in destroying rather than protecting the heritage.

  • Court must not be misleaded by any party: The government has lied, deceived and misleaded the court by saying that no decision has been for constructing road through the park though it has made number of correspondence for it. It is not righteous to mislead the court by any party.

SUMMARY
Prem Raj Bhatt had been appointed as the director of the National Insurance Corporation for a period of 3 years by a ministerial decision of the Government of Nepal. The petitioner was also assigned the responsibility of Assistant Administrator until further notice. After about 7 months, the petitioner has been removed from the post of Assistant Administrator and another person has been appointed to the post by the government. Prem Raj Bhatt filed a writ petition in the Supreme Court with claiming that the government decision was taken against the principle of natural justice without giving him a chance to be heard and also the government violated his fundamental right of employment. The Supreme Court dismissed the writ petition saying that the petitioner was not removed from his post but was removed from the responsibility of ad hoc nature and in such a case it is not necessary to give a chance to be heard.

VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
The writ petition was quashed: The apex court quashed the writ petition stating that the claim of the petitioner that he should remain in the the assistant administrator post forever is not justifiable as theresponsibility assigned to him was of temporary nature.

  • Adhocly given responsibility can be wihdrawn adhocly: The applicant was not selected from any competition by demonstrating his ability and efficiency. The responsibility of the Assistant Administrator has been assigned to the petitioner on an ad hoc basis till further arrangements. Such responsibility can be withdrawn at any time.

  • Withdrawing a person from the multiple responsibilities does not violate the Right to employment : The petitioner has not been relieved from the responsibility of the director of the corporation but only from the responsibility of the assistant administrator.

  • Hiring authority has Firing authority: In the event that the term of office is not clearly defined, the body which has the right to assign the responsibility in the same manner shall be able to remove the responsibility in the same manner.

  • Exception to the Principles of Natural Justice: Unless otherwise clearly specified by law, it is not necessary to give a hearing opportunity to remove a person from the responsibility of ad hoc nature.

SUMMARY
Defendant has measured the land (which was in the sole possession of the plaintiff) in his own name during the new survey. The Land Revenue Office registered the land in the name of the defendant without understanding the plaintiff on the basis of the very survey gossip (paper). The plaintiff demanded that the survey and registration in the name of the defendant should be canceled and should be registered in his name. The dispute went upto Supreme Court. The plaintiff won the case.
RATIO & PRECEDENTS
  • No notification no limitation: Since the plaintiff did not know on the spot that the defendant had taken the land ownership certificate (Lalpurja) in his name at the time of the new survey, the plantiff could not be said to be out of date.

  • Principle of Fair Hearing:
    The act of registering the land in the name of another person without notifying the owner of the land should be considered as corrupt.

  • Corrupt Decision doesn’t obtain Legal Recognition: The action and decision taken to change the land plot number (kitta number) and the area of the land without any basis and evidence is a corrupt decision. Such corrupt decision doesn’t obtain legal recognition.

    • SUMMARY

According to the agreement, Tularatna made a bond paper and also gave a bond of 3 lakh rupees to Chetsundar. After receiving his share, Chetsundar refused to sell the property. After that, Tularatna filed a case against Chetsundar in the Lalitpur District Court to enforce the contract as usual, or if it is not possible to enforce it as usual, to pay double the amount of the bond (Rs. 6 lakh) as compensation. It was decided by the district court that Chetsundar will be liable to pay the principal and interest (Rs. 3 lakh principal and interest at the rate of 10% not exceeding the principal amount) to Tularatna. Dissatisfied with that decision, Tularatna appealed to the Appellate Court Patan (now High Court Patan). The Appellate Court of Patan upheld the decision of the District Court. Challenging the decision, Tularatna filed a case revision petition in the Supreme Court. After granting the permission for revision, the court ordered that the case be submitted to the larger full bench. The opinion of the majority (6:1) was upheld.
    VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
    The decision of the appellate court will be upheld. (Note: Disagreeing with the reasons and principles taken by the majority of judges to reach the decision and conclusion, although the minority has a separate opinion, both of them have concluded to uphold the decision of the Court of Appeal.)
    RATIO & PRECEDENTS (Majority Opinion):
    • Promises Should be Kept: Our contract law was born and developed in the ancient moral and religious foundation of being honest with one's word.

    • Unregistered deed is only an agreement, not a contract:
      An unregistered contract cannot be considered a contract as it cannot be enforced by law. That would be just an agreement. 3.

    • Efficient Remedy cannot be obtained on the basis of premature deeds: The court cannot order the transfer of immovable property on the basis of an immature deed (deed that has not been registered).

    • Inconsistent and discordant previous precedents will be null and void: All principles propounded by the Supreme Court (including principle established in D.No 7915) that the right of immovable property can be transferred through a unregistered deed of transfer will be invalid and ineffective from the very date.

    Principle propounded in Minority Opinion
    • A deed of transfer although not registered is a contract: A deed of transfer prepared with mutual consent is a contract although not registered as mutual obligations and returns are determined between the parties. Based on that, the ownership of immovable property can also be transferred.

    • Compensation is Rule and Specific Performance is Exception: In case of breach of contract the general rule is that the innocent party is entitled with compensation. Remedy of Specific Performance is only an exception in case of breach of contract.

The company entered into an agreement with the Postal Services Department to complete the construction of the Postal Services Department building within 24 months. However, he pointed out problems such as blockades and earthquakes, saying that the work would not be completed on time, and he was given an additional 26 months. However, the construction of the building could not be completed on time. As per the terms of the agreement for unnecessary delays, the Department of Postal Services imposed Liquidated Damage (i.e, 0.05% of the contract amount will be paid daily as compensation for the delay). The petitioner company then appointed a arbitrator on its behalf to resolve the dispute through the Dispute Adjudication Board and also wrote to the Opposition Postal Service Department to appoint a arbitrator on its behalf. However, the Department of Postal Services did not appoint an arbitrator on its behalf. The petitioner company applied to the Patan High Court to appoint one arbitrator from the court for the opposition Postal Service Department as per Section 7 of the Arbitration Act, 2055 BS. But the Patan High Court refused to appoint an arbitrator. There was no provision in the law to appeal against such decision of the Patan High Court. Therefore, the petitioner company entered the Supreme Court with a writ petition of certiorari / mandamus to overturn the decision of the High Court Patan. The Supreme Court quashed the writ petition, refusing to issue a writ.

    • Contract Confines The Court:
      Unless the agreement clearly states that disputes will be settled by arbitration, the court itself cannot add new conditions to resolve disputes through arbitration.

    नेपाल सरकारको सडक विभाग र स्वच्छन्द सूर्या जे.भी.बीच चैनपुर खाँदवारी सडक स्तरोन्नति गर्ने गरी करार सम्झौता भयो। सडक विभागले सुझाएको (साइट नजिकको) अरूण नदीको ढुङ्गाजन्य पदार्थ प्रयोगशालामा परीक्षण गर्दा मापदण्ड विपरीत भयो। निर्माण साइट भन्दा करिव १६५ कि.मि टाढा रहेको गाछीया नदिबाट नदिजन्य पदार्थ ल्याई ठेकेदार कम्पनीले सडक स्तरोन्नति कार्य सुरू गर्यो। सो कार्यको निम्ति ठेकेदार कम्पनीले करारको म्याद थप र थप क्षतिपूर्तिको माग गर्‍यो। तर सडक विभागले ठेकेदार कम्पनिको मागलाई अस्वीकार गर्‍यो। तर सो विवाद मध्यस्थताबाट निरूपण गर्न भने दुवै पक्ष राजी भए। मध्यस्थ ट्राइबुनलले सडक विभागले ठेकेदार कम्पनिलाई करिव ७ करोड क्षतिपूर्ति दिनुपर्ने र १२५ दिनको म्याद थप गर्नुपर्ने भनी निर्णय (अवार्ड) गर्‍यो। सो निर्णयमा चित्त नबुझाई नेपाल सरकार, सडक विभागले पुनरावेदन अदालत पाटनमा निवेदन दियो। मध्यस्थता ट्राइबुनलले अधिकारक्षेत्र भन्दा बाहिर गई निर्णय गरेकोले पुनः निर्णय गर्नु भनी मध्यस्थकर्ताको नाममा आदेश जारी गरी पाउँ भन्ने नै निवेदनको मूख्य माग रहेको थियो। तर पुनरावेदन अदालतले मध्यस्थ ट्राइबुनलको निर्णय नै सदर गर्‍यो। पुनरावेदन अदालतको सो निर्णयले राज्यलाई करौडौको आर्थिक दायित्व थुपारेको भन्दै सो निर्णय बदर गराउन सडक विभागले उत्प्रेषणको रिट (वैकल्पिक उपचारको मार्ग नभएकोले) सर्वोच्च अदालतमा दायर गर्‍यो। रिट खारेज हुने ठहर्‍याई सर्वोच्च अदालतबाट आदेश (फैसला) भयो।
    निर्णयाधार र प्रतिपादित सिद्धान्त
    • करारका दुवै पक्षको हैसियत बराबर हुनेः नेपाल सरकार वा त्यसको कुनै निकायले कुनै पक्षसँग व्यवसायिक प्रकृतिको करार गर्दछ भने दुवै पक्षको हैसियत बराबर नै हुन्छ। जनताले तिरेको करबाट भुक्तानी दिनुपर्ने भन्दैमा सरकारले विशेष अनुकम्पा र सहानुभूति नपाउने।

    • सरकार करारको पक्ष भएको बेला सार्वभौम शक्तिको प्रयोग गर्न नसक्ने: करार गर्दा सरकारी निकायले पनि व्यक्तिसरहकै अधिकार प्रयोग गर्नुपर्ने। सरकारले करारको शर्तबाहेक सार्वभौम विशेषाधिकार (sovereign right) को प्रयोग गर्न नपाउने।

    SUMMARY
    A contract was signed between the Road Department of the Government of Nepal and Swachhand Surya J.V. to upgrade the Chainpur Khandwari road. Laboratory testing of Arun River materials (near the site) which was recommended by the Department of Road was found to be against the specified standard. The contractor company started the road upgradation work by bringing river materials from the Gachhia river, which is about 165 km away from the construction site. For that work, the contractor company demanded an extension of the contract and additional compensation. But the road department rejected the demand of the contractor company. But both parties agreed to settle the dispute through arbitration. The arbitral tribunal decided (awarded) that the road department should compensate the contractor company with around 70 million and extend the deadline by 125 days. Dissatisfied with that decision, the Nepal Government, the Road Department filed an application in the Appeallate Court Patan. The main demand of the petition was to issue an order in the name of the arbitrator to reconsider the decision as the arbitral tribunal had decided beyond its jurisdiction. But the appellate court upheld the decision of the arbitral tribunal. The Roads Department filed a writ of certiorari (as there was no alternative remedy) in the Supreme Court to overturn the decision of the Apeallate Court stating that the Court has imposed huge amount of financial liability which should be paid through the tax collected from public. The Supreme Court dismissed the writ petition.
    RATIO & PRECEDENTS
    • The status of both parties to the contract shall be equal: If the Government of Nepal or any of its agencies enters into a commercial nature contract with any party, the status of both parties shall be equal. The government will not get special sympathy and sympathy for saying that it should be paid from the taxes paid by the people.

    • Government cannot be privileged to use sovereign power while being a party to a contract:
      While contracting, government agencies should exercise the same rights as individuals. The government cannot exercise sovereign right besides the terms of the agreement.

    SUMMARY

About 2 & 1/2 years after the decision, Sunil Kumar Adhikari (Tenant’s son) filed a writ petition in the appellate court. The appellate court quashed the writ petition stating that he had entered the court with undue delay. The petitioner appealed to the Supreme Court. The Supreme Court overturned the decision of appeallate court & also quashed the decision of the Land Reform Office stating that the decision was reached in contrary to the principle of natural justice. Supreme Court also issued an order in the name of the Land Reform Office to redecide the case by providing an opportunity of hearing to both parties.

    VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
    SC overturned the of judgment of Appellate Court. It also anulled the decison of Land Office through the writ of Certiorari. Supreme Court issued the Mandamus in the name of the Land Reforms Office to redecide the case by giving the opportunity of hearing to both party.
    RATIO & PRECEDENTS
    • Laches and Limitation are not the same thing: The principle of laches cannot be applied through the technical calculation of the time as in limitation.

    • The following things should be considered to conclude the laches (delay):
      1) The nature of the issue
      2) The nature of the proceedings of the case
      3) The time taken by the petitioner to come to the judicial body
      4) The effect produced in the rights and interests of other person when the remedy is provided to the petitioner.
      5) Whether the purpose of justice is fulfilled or not.

    • Burden to Justify Laches: The party that raises the issue of laches should himself justify it.

    • No participation no information: As the Land Reform Office did not involve the petitioner in the decision making process, it cannot be said that the petitioner was aware of the decision in advance.

    • During Gross Injustice and Miscarriage of Justice petition can be filed under extraordinary jurisdiction: If the rights of someone else is frustrated through a decision made in contrary to the universal principle of law and justice then the person can bring writ petititon under extraordinary jurisdiction despite the existence of alternative remedy.

    • Principle of fair hearing: A decision made without providing an opportunity of hearing to the affected party is against the principle of natural justice.

    सर्वोच्च अदालतको आदेश (फैसला)
    पीडिताको बयान अदालतबाट प्रमाणित भएतापनि पीडिता स्वयं अदालतमा उपस्थित नभएको अवस्थामा सो प्रमाणलाई अकाट्य र निर्णायक प्रमाण मान्न नमिल्ने हुँदा पुनरावेदन अदालतको फैसला उल्टी हुने र प्रतिवादीहरूले सफाई पाउने भनी सर्वोच्च अदालतबाट फैसला भयो।
    निर्णयाधार र प्रतिपादित सिद्धान्त
    • पीडितको बयानलाई अदालतबाट प्रमाणित गरिएको भन्दै सोलाई अकाट्य बयान मान्न नमिल्नेः पीडितको बयान अदालतबाट प्रमाणित गराउँदै बयान आफैँमा एक महत्त्वपूर्ण प्रमाणको रूपमा रहे तापनि सो बयान अन्य प्रमाणबाट समर्थित हुनुपर्ने। मुद्दाको कारबाहीको सिलसिलामा पीडित अदालतमा उपस्थित नभएमा अदालतबाट प्रमाणित भएको निजको बयानलाई अकाट्य प्रमाण (irrefutable evidence) र निर्णायक प्रमाण (conclusive evidence) को रूपमा अदालतले ग्रहण गर्न नमिल्ने।

    • सुनिजाने प्रमाण प्रमाण नहुनेः
      वादी तर्फका साक्षीहरू प्रत्यक्षदर्शी नभएको र सुनेको आधारमा अदालतमा आई बकपत्र गरेको हुँदा उनीहरूको भनाई सुनिजान्‍ने प्रमाण (hearsay evidence) रहेको र यस्तो सुनिजान्‍ने प्रमाणको आधारमा मात्र कसुरदार कायम गरिनु उचित नहुने।
    SUMMARY
    The victim V has lodged a complaint with the police alleging that the defendants P1 and P2 sold him to India after being tempted to work at the border. In this regard, the police took a statement from the victim and also verified her statement from the court. Defendants P1 and P2 were also arrested and the incident was investigated. A charge sheet had been filed in the district court seeking legal action against the defendants for committing crimes against human trafficking. The district court found the defendants guilty and sentenced them according to the claim made in the charge sheet. The decision of the district court was also been uphold by the appellate court . Defendants had appealed to the Supreme Court pleading to overturn the appellate court's decision and acquit them of the charges. The Supreme Court acquitted the defendants.
    VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
    The decision of apellate court is overturned & defendents are aquitted on the grounds that even if the victim's statement in police was verified by the court, it cannot be considered as irrefutable and conclusive evidence as the victim failed to be present in the court to verify her statement.
    RATIO & PRECEDENTS
    • Evidences must support each other: Although a court-certified statement is an important piece of evidence in itself, it must be supported by other evidence.

    • Statement made by victim infront of investigation authority though certified by court is not conclusive evidence:
      If the victim does not appear before the court in connection with the proceedings of the case, his statement attested by the court cannot be accepted by the court as irrefutable and conclusive evidence.

    • Hearsay evidence is no evidence: As the plaintiffs' witnesses were not eyewitnesses and had come to the court on the basis of what they heard, their statements were hearsay evidence and it would not be appropriate to convict them on the basis of such evidence only.

    निर्णयाधार र प्रतिपादित सिद्धान्त
    • सार्वजनिक हितको निम्ति कार्य गर्दा समेत कानूनी प्रकृया पूरा गर्नुपर्नेः सडक विस्तार गर्ने कार्य सार्वजनिक हितको कार्य भएको र यस्तोमा जग्गा दिन्न भन्ने अधिकार निवेदिकाहरुलाई नहुने। तर विधिसम्मत प्रक्रियाद्वारा जग्गा प्राप्तिबारे निर्णय गरेर सडक विस्तार हुनुपर्ने भन्ने निवेदिकाहरूको जिकिर संविधानसम्मत रहेको।

    • जनताको अधिकारको रक्षा गर्नु अदालतले अभिभावकीय जिम्मेवारी हुनेः आयोजनाको (सडक) निम्ति जग्गा प्राप्त गरिसकेपछि असर परेको जनतालाई विर्सदै जाने प्रवृतिको पक्षमा अदालत रहन नसक्ने। जनताहरू अन्यायमा परेको र कानुनी उपचारविहिन अवस्थामा रहेको कुरा अदालतले टुलुटुलु हेरेर बस्न नसक्ने।

    • क्षतिपूर्तिको कार्य र सडक विस्तारको कार्य सँगसँगै लैजानुपर्नेः क्षतिपू्र्ति उपलब्ध गराए पछि मात्र जग्गा प्राप्त गर्नुपर्छ भन्ने धारणा अदालतको नरहेको। क्षतिपूर्ति उपलब्ध गराउने र आयोजना निर्माण एवम् विस्तारको कार्य सँगसँगै लगेमात्र संविधानद्वारा प्रत्याभूत सम्पति र आवासको हकको रक्षा हुन सक्ने।

    • सरकारले गुण्डागर्दी शैलीमा कार्य गर्न नसुहाउनेः नागरिकको सम्पति अपहरण हुने वा खोसिने विषयमा सरकार संवेदनशील हुनपर्ने। गुण्डा शैलीमा असंवेदनशील ढङ्गले कार्य गर्न नमिल्ने।

    • “धर्मो रक्षति रक्षित”: विधिको रक्षा गरियो भने मात्र हाम्रो रक्षा हुन सक्ने। यो “धर्मो रक्षति रक्षितः” भने जस्तै कुरा हो। संविधानद्वारा संस्थापित हकहरुको विरूद्ध जाने अधिकार कसैलाई नहुने।
    SUMMARY
    The Kathmandu Valley Development Authority (KVDA) had forcibly demolishing houses and ocsescupying land without fulfilling the rules and procedures in the name of widening the road for the public interest. The petitioners were outraged by the KVDA action and had taken refuge in the Supreme Court seeking justice through a writ petition. Their main demand was to issue an order in the name of the opposition not to do any work related to road expansion without compensation. The Supreme Court had ordered that the work of compensation should not be delayed and the work of road widening should be done in unison.
    VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT:
    As no decision has been made for not paying compensation to the petitioners, the order of certiorari have not been issued. Therefore, the Supreme Court ruled that a Mandamus will be issued in the name of the opponents to make the road widening work only after making a decision on compensation.
    RATIO DECIDENDI AND ESTABLISHED PRECEDENT:
    • Eminent Domain should be exercised in accordance to the law: The work of widening the road is an act of public interest and the petitioners do not have the right for not giving land for such works. However, the petitioners' main insistence was that the decision on land acquisition be taken by following a due process of law. Such claim of petitioner is a valid and reliable claim.


    • Court must act as guardian to protect people’s right: The court cannot be in favor of the tendency of forgetting the affected people after acquiring land for the project (road). Also, the court cannot sit idly by and see that the people are being treated unfairly and without legal redress.


    • Procedure of Acquisition of land and Compensation procedure should be carried out simultaneously: The court did not hold the view that the land should be acquired only after providing compensation. The right to property and housing guaranteed by the constitution can be protected only if the work of providing compensation and project work is carried out hand in hand.


    • Government cannot act as dacoit: The government should be sensitive about the abduction or snatching of citizens' property.


    • We are protected only if law is protected: We can only be protected if the law is protected.

      ii) Whether the lower court proceedings are within legal framework or not?

    मुद्दा तथा रिट महाशाखाबाट खडा गरिएको प्रतिवेदनको सारसङ्क्षेप
    विश्वभरी महामारीको रूपमा फैलिदैं गरेको कोरोना भाइरस (कोभिड-१९) को सङ्क्रमण नेपालमा समेत फैलिएको छ। सङ्क्रमणलाई कम गर्न नेपाल सरकारले Lockdown घोषणा गरी सोको निरन्तर रूपमा म्याद थप गरेको अवस्था छ। हदम्याद, म्याद र तारेख गुज्रेको अवस्थामा थाम्न मिल्ने कानूनहरू भएता पनि Lockdown को अवस्थाले गुज्रेकोमा भने ती कानून अपर्याप्त त अव्यवहारिक छन्। जस्तैः एकै पटक राज्यभर बन्दाबन्दी नखुली आंशिक क्षेत्रमा खुलेमा र सर्वत्र यातायात सुचारू नभएको अवस्थामा म्याद, तारेख र हदम्यादको गणना कसरी गर्ने? बन्दाबन्दी खुल्नासाथ अदालतमा भिडभाड थप बढ्न सक्ने हुँदा त्यस अवस्था के-कसरी सेवा प्रवाह गर्ने? कसूरदार ठहर भएको तीस दिनभित्र सजाय निर्धारण गर्नुपर्ने कानूनी व्यवस्था रहेकोमा बन्दाबन्दीका अवस्थामा त्यस्तो कानूनको कार्यान्वयन के- कसरी गर्ने भन्ने जस्ता समस्याहरूको मुद्दा तथा रिट महाशाखाले प्रतिवेदन खडा गरी त्यस्ता अडबड निकासा (समाधान)को लागि इजलास समक्ष पेश गर्‍यो।
    अधिवक्ता टिकाराम भट्टराईले दायर गरेको रिट निवेदनको सारसङ्क्षेप
    विश्वव्यापी रूपमा फैलिएको कोराना भाइरसको (कोभिड-१९) को कारण नेपाल सरकारले घोषणा गरेको लकडाउनका कारण एकातर्फ अदालतको सेवा समेत प्रभावित हुन पुगेको छ भने अर्कातर्फ यही अवधिमा नागरिकका मौलिक हक तथा कानूनी हक संरक्षण गर्ने दायित्वबाट न्यायपालिका विमुख हुन हुँदैन। यस बन्दाबन्दीले पक्षहरूको म्याद, हदम्याद र तारिखसमेत प्रभावित भएको छ। हाल प्रचलनमा रहेका कानून यस्तो असामान्य अवस्थालाई सम्बोधन गर्न अप्रयाप्त रहेकोले ती कानूनी रिक्तताको स्थिति आएको छ। हिडडुलमा नै रोक लगाएको अवस्था हुँदा कानून व्यवसायीहरू अदालतसम्म आइपुगेर बहस पैरवी गर्ने अवस्था छेन। अतः यी समस्याको निराकरण गर्नको निम्ति जो जसलाई आवश्य पर्छ सो निकाय र व्यक्ति उपर परमादेश जारी होस् भनी विपक्षी विहिन (निवेदक रहेको तर विपक्षी कोही नभएको) रिट निवेदन सर्वोच्च अदालतमा दर्ता भयो। सर्वोच्च अदालतले Amicus Curiae को सहयोग लिई निवेदन र प्रतिवेदन दुवैलाई किनारा लगायो।
    सर्वोच्च अदालतको आदेशः
    सर्वोच्च अदालतको १९ जना न्यायाधीशको वृहत पूर्ण इजलासले महामारी विधिशास्त्र सम्बन्धी मौलिक सिद्धान्तहरू प्रतिपादन गर्‍यो।
    निर्णयाधार र प्रतिपादित सिद्धान्त
    • फौजदारी मुद्दामा पनि हदम्याद थाम्न सकिनेः काबु बाहिरको परिस्थिति परेको अवस्थामा हदम्याद थाम्न सकिने देवानी संहिताको कानूनलाई विस्तार गरी फौजादारी विवादमा पनि लागु गर्न सकिने।

    • कानूनको व्याख्याको माध्यमबाट सम्बोधन गर्न नसकिने जटिलताहरू कानून निर्माण गरेर भएपनि अदालतले सम्बोधन गर्नसक्नेः
      संविधानले सक्षम न्यायपालिकाको परिकल्पना गरेको हुँदा कानूनी रिक्तताको कारणबाट उत्पन्न उपचारविहिनताको अवस्था हट्ने गरी कानून निर्माण गर्न पाउने अदालतको अन्तरनिहीत अधिकार हुने।

    • न्यायिक निकायको पहुँचमा अवरोध हुनु भनेको स्वच्छ सुनुवाईको हकमा आघात पुग्नु होः व्यक्तिको आफ्नै दोष, कमजोरी र लापर्वाही बाहेक अन्य अवस्थामा स्वच्छ सुनुवाइको हकमा आघात पुर्‍याउन नहुने।

    • प्रतिकूल परिस्थितमा पनि न्यायको ढोका खुल्ला रहनुपर्नेः कानूनी अड्चन देखाएर न्यायका सेवाग्राहीको अपेक्षाहरूलाई अलपत्र छोड्न नहुने

    • बन्दाबन्दीको अवस्थालाई शून्य अवधि मान्नेः नियमित सेवा प्रवाह बन्द गरिएको मितिदेखि बन्दाबन्दी खुलेको मितिसम्मको अवधिलाई शून्य अवधि (zero period मानी) सो अवधिलाई हदम्याद, म्याद, तारिखको लागि गणना नगर्ने।

    SUMMARY of the Report of Case and Writ Division of SC:
    The corona virus (Covid-19) which is spreading like an pandemic all over the world has also spread in Nepal. In order to reduce the infection, the government of Nepal has announced a lockdown and has continuously extended it. Although there exists law in the case of the expiry of the limitation, deadline and date, those laws are insufficient and impractical during the situtation of lockdown. For example: How to calculate the deadline & date if the closure is not opened in the entire state and the traffic is not smooth everywhere? As soon as the closure is opened, the crowd in the court may increase, so how will the service be provided in that situation? As there is a legal provision to make sentence hearing within thirty days of conviction hearing, what is the implementation of such law in the case of lockdown? The case and writ division of Supreme Court prepared a report and submitted it to the bench for the resolution of such problems.
    Summary of Writ Petition filed by Adv.TikaRam Bhattarai:
    Court services have also been affected due to the lockdown announced by the Nepalese government due to the global spread of the Corona virus (Covid-19). But during this period, the responsibility of protecting the fundamental rights and legal rights of the citizens should be performed more strongly than before by the judiciary. Due to this lockdown, the limitation, deadlines and dates of presence of the parties have also been affected. ' A legal vacuum has arisen as the existing laws are inadequate to deal with such unusual situations. There is no situation for legal professionals to come to the court and plead the case when there is a ban in movement. Therefore, in order to solve these problems, an unopposed ( only petitioner without opponents) writ petition was registered in the Supreme Court to issue Mandamus against the entities and individuals to whom court finds necessary.
    VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
    The Supreme Court Propounded the Pandemic Jurisprudence uanimously by a larger full bench of 19 Justices.
    RATIO & PRECEDENTS
    • The statute of limitations can also be extended in criminal cases: The limitation which can be extended in the case of uncontrollable circumstances as per Civil Code can also be extended and applied to criminal disputes as well.

    • Complications that cannot be addressed through the interpretation of the law can be addressed by the courts through the creation of laws:
      As the constitution envisages a competent judiciary, the courts have the inherent right to make laws so as to addrress the situation of lack of remedy due to legal vacancies.

    • Obstruction of access to a judicial body is a violation of the right to a fair hearing: A person's right to a fair hearing should not be violated by any other reasons other than his own fault, weakness and negligence.

    • The door of justice should remain open even in adverse circumstances: The expectations of the customers of justice should not be left in abeyance by showing legal obstacles

    • The state of lockdown shall be treated as zero period: The period from the date of stoppage of regular service to the date of opening of the closure shall be considered as zero period.

    सारसङ्क्षेप
    भीमबहादुर नेपाली उपर आफ्नै भाइबुहारीलाई जबजस्ती करणी गरेको भनी हाडनाता जबरजस्ती करणी आरोपमा तेह्रथुम जिल्ला अदालतमा अभियोगपत्र दायर भयो। जिल्ला अदालतले भीमबहादुर नेपालीलाई कसूरदार ठहर गरी जवरजस्ती करणीको महल बमोजिम ७ वर्ष कैद र हाडनाता करणी गर्नेको महल बमोजिम ६ वर्ष कैद गरी जम्मा १३ वर्ष कैद हुने र पीडितलाई १०,०००।-(दश हजार) क्षतिपूर्ति भराइ दिने ठहर्‍याई फैसला गर्‍यो। हामीले पहिले देखिनै करणी लिनु दिनु गर्दै आएका थियौ, त्यस दिन पनि हामीले सहमतिमा नै करणी लिनु दिनु गरेका हौं, मैले जबर्जस्ती करणी गरेको होइन भनी भीमबहादुर नेपालीले पुनरावेदन अदालत धनकुटामा पुनरावेदन गरे। पुनरावेदन अदालत धनकुटाले जिल्ला अदालतको फैसलालाई सदर गर्‍यो। मैले जबरजस्ती करणी गरेको भन्ने चिन्हहरू के-के थिए भन्ने खुल्न नआएको र अदालतलाई सहयोग पुग्ने गरी यथार्थ बयान गरेको अवस्थामा मलाई न्युनतम हदसम्मको सजाय हुनुपर्ने हो, उपल्लो हदको सजाय हुनुपर्ने होइन भनी प्रतिवादी भीमबहादुर नेपालीले सर्वोच्च अदालतमा पुनरावेदन गरे। सर्वोच्च अदालतले पुनरावेदन अदालत धनकुटाको फैसला नै सदर गर्‍यो।
    सर्वोच्च अदालतको फैसला
    प्रतिवादीले एकातिर जबरजस्ती करणी गरेको नै होइन भन्ने जिकिर लिने र अर्को तर्फ जबजस्ती करणीमा न्यूनतम सजायको दावी गरी विरोधि कुरा गरेको एवं विगतमा समेत निजको आचरण खराब रहेको देखिएको भन्दै सर्वोच्च अदालतले निजलाई न्युनतम हदको सजाय गर्न इन्कार गरी पुनरावेदन अदालत धनकुटाको फैसलालाई नै सदर गर्‍यो।
    SUMMARY
    A charged sheet had been filed in the Tehrathum District Court against Bhim Bahadur Nepali for allegedly raping his own sister-in-law. The district court found Bhim Bahadur Nepali guilty and sentenced him to 7 years imprisonment as per the Chapter of the Rape and 6 years imprisonment as per the Chapter of Incest.Defendent Bhim Bahadur Nepali has appealed to the Appellate Court Dhankuta saying that there is no offence of rape as we have been indulged in sex from the very beginning by our mutual consent. The Appellate Court Dhankuta upheld the decision of the district court. Defendant Bhim Bahadur Nepali appealed to the Supreme Court that he should be punished to a minimum and not to a higher degree, because firstly no signs of rape are disclosed and secondly he had stated the facts in a way that would help the court. The Supreme Court upheld the decision of Supreme Court.
    VERDICT OF SUPREME COURT
    The Supreme Court denied administering the minimum degree of punishment as the defendents words were contradictory.On the one hand, he claimed that he had not committed any act of rape and on the other hand, he spoke against the claim by pleading for minimum punishment in the offence of rape.The SC thus upheld the decision of Appeallate Court.

    वादी नेपाल सरकारको तर्फबाट पेश हुने बहसनोट/ पुनरावलोकनको निवेदनको निम्ति सहयोगी बुँदाहरुः
    • प्रतिवादीले आफूले पीडित महिलालाई करणी गरेको भनी स्वीकार गरेका छन्। रातको समयमा ढोका फोरी घिसार्दै जङ्गलतर्फ लगी कपडा च्यातचुत गरी करणी भएको हुँदा यस्तो करणीलाई प्रतिवादीले भने जस्तो सहमतिमा भएको भन्न मिल्दैन।

    • पीडितालाई ढोका फोरी जबजस्ती जङ्गलतर्फ लैजादै गरेको देखेका प्रत्यक्षदर्शी भनिएका पीडिताका छोरीहरूले आफूहरुले मौकामा लेखाएको कुरालाई समर्थन हुने गरी अदालतमा वकपत्र गरेका छन्।

    • पीडिता स्वयंले जाहेरी दिएकी छिन् र आफ्नो जाहेरी वेहोरालाई समर्थन हुने गरी अदालतमा आई वकपत्र समेत गरेकी छिन्।

    • प्रतिवादीले करणी गरेपश्चात भागेका छन्। जबरजस्ती करणी नगरेको भए भाग्ने र पुलिसबाट लुकी छिपि हिड्ने अवस्था नै आउँदैनथ्यो।

    • प्रतिवादी स्वयंले जबजस्ती करणी उद्योगमा एक पटक सजाय पाइसकेकोले विगतमा निजको आचरण खराब रहेको देखिन्छ। यसबाट प्रतिवादीले सहमतिमा करणी गरेको भन्ने जिकिर पत्यारलाग्दो देखिंदैन। निज प्रतिवादीलाई जबजस्ती करणी र हाडनाता करणी दुवैमा सजाय गरेको फैसला मिलेको छ। अतः तल्लो अदालतको फैसला नै सदर हुनुपर्छ।

    प्रतिवादीको तर्फबाट पेश हुने पुनरावेदन/पुनरावलोकन निवेदनको निम्ति सहयोगी बुँदाहरूः
    • प्रतिवादीले पहिले देखिनै सहमतिमा करणी लिनु दिनु गरी आएको कुरा व्यक्त गरेका छन्। उक्त दिन पनि सहमतिमा नै करणी भएको हो भनी अनुसन्धानको क्रममा र अदालती वयानको क्रममा समेत व्यक्त भएको छ। साबालिकसँग सहमतिमा भएको करणीलाई जबरजस्ती करणी भन्न मिल्दैन।

    • साक्षीको रूपमा अदालतमा बकपत्र गर्ने जाहेरवालिका छोरीहरु नाबालक रहेको र उनीहरुले जे सिकायो त्यही कुरा व्यक्त गर्ने गर्दछन्। अतः त्यस्ता तोते बोलीलाई प्रमाणमा लिन मिल्दैन।

    • परिवर्तित नाम जमुनाकुमारीले आफ्नो श्रीमानको भय र समाजको डरको कारणले सहमतिमा भएको करणीलाई जबरजस्ती करणी हो भनी जाहेरी दिने र सोही अनुरुप बकपत्र गरेको हुँदा यसलाई प्रमाणमा लिन मिल्दैन।

    • गाउँलेहरूको आवाज सुनेपछि जाहेरवालीले नै भाग भनेपछि प्रतिवादी भागेको भन्ने देखिन्छ। जाहेरवालीले नै भगाएको हुँदा उक्त करणी सहमतिमा नै भएको देखिन्छ।

    सारसङ्क्षेप
    रातिको समयमा बाटोमा बस कुरिरहेको अवस्थामा भूपेन्द्र घिमिरे र सन्तोष कार्कीमाथि खुकुरी प्रहार गरी गम्भीर चोट पुर्‍याए। खुकुरी प्रहार गर्ने को थिए भन्ने तत्काल पत्ता लगाउन भने सकिएन। स्थानीय मानिसहरू मिलि घाइतेहरुलाई भने अस्पताल पुर्‍याए। भूपेन्द्र घिमिरेको सोहि चोटका कारण उसै राती उपचारको क्रममा मृत्यु भयो भने सन्तोष कार्की भने निको भए। भूपेन्द्र घिमिरेका बाबुले किटानि जाहेरी दिएतापनि तत्काल सो घटनामा कुनै आरोपितहरू प्रक्राउ नपरेको कारण थप अनुसन्धान हुन सकेन। घटना घटेको करिव ६ वर्ष पछि मात्र विष्णु याख्खा (राई) प्रक्राउ परे। निज उपर अनुसन्धान कार्य भएपश्चात विष्णु याख्खा (राई) समेत ४ जनालाई कर्तव्य ज्यानको कसूर गरेको आरोप लगाई मोरङ जिल्ला अदालतमा अभियोगपत्र दायर भयो। जिल्ला अदालतले विष्णु याख्खा राईलाई कसूरदार ठहर गरी जन्मकैदको फैसला गर्‍यो। अन्य ३ जना प्रतिवादी म्यादमा हाजिर नभई फरार रहेकोले निजहरूको हकमा भने मुद्दा मुलतवीमा राखियो। वादी नेपाल सरकारले प्रतिवादी विष्णु याख्खा राईलाई जन्मकैद मात्र होइन सर्वस्व सहित जन्मकैद गर्नुपर्छ भनी र प्रतिवादी विष्णु याख्खा राईले आफूले आरोपित कसूरबाट सफाई हुनुपर्ने भन्दै वादी प्रतिवादी दुवैले पुनरावेदन अदालत विराटनगरमा पुनरावेदन गरे। यसैवीच मुलतवी रहेको मुद्दा जगाई तीनै जना प्रतिवादीलाई सफाई हुने गरी जिल्ला अदालतबाट अर्को फैसला भयो। यस फैसलामा पनि चित्त नबुझाई वादी नेपाल सरकारले पुनरावेदन अदालत विराटनगरमा पुनरावेदन गर्‍यो। अघि पछि गरी वादी नेपाल सरकारको तर्फबाट परेका २ पुनरावेदनपत्र र प्रतिवादी विष्णु याख्खा राईको तर्फबाट परेको १ पुनरावेदनपत्र गरी ३ पुनरावेदपत्रलाई एक साथ राखी पुनरावेदन अदालत विराटनगरले सुनुवाइ गर्‍यो। विष्णु याख्खा राईलाई कसुरदार ठहर्‌याई जन्मकैदको सजाय गरेको जिल्ला अदालतको फैसला मिलेको छ भनी एक न्यायाधीशले राय व्यक्त गरे भने विष्णु याख्खा राईले अभियोगदावीबाट सफाई पाउने हुँदा जिल्ला अदालतको फैसला सोहदसम्म उल्टी हुने भन्ने राय व्यक्त गरी अर्का न्यायाधीशले फैसला गरे। यसरी पुनरावेदन अदालतका संयुक्त इजलासका न्यायाधीशवीच राय बाझिएपछि तेस्रो न्यायाधीशकोमा पेश भयो। तेस्रो न्यायाधीशले विष्णु कुमार याख्खा राईलाई सफाई हुने ठहर्‍याई व्यक्त भएको रायलाई नै सदर गरे। आफ्नो पुनरावेदन जिकिर पुग्न नसकेपछि वादी नेपाल सरकारले पुनरावेदन अदालत विराटनगरको फैसलालाई चुनौति दिंदै सर्वोच्च अदालतमा पुनरावेदन गरे। सर्वोच्च अदालतले पुनरावेदन अदालत विराटनगरको फैसला मिलेको हुँदा सदर हुने, वादी नेपाल सरकारको पुनरावेदन जिकिर पुग्न नसक्ने ठहर्‍याई फैसला गर्‍यो।
    SUMMARY
    While waiting for the bus on the road at night, Bhupendra Ghimire and Santosh Karki were attacked with khukuri and seriously injured. It was not immediately clear who attacked with the khukuri. Locals rushed the injured to the hospital. Bhupendra Ghimire died on the same night due to the very injury while Santosh Karki recovered. Although Bhupendra Ghimire's father had lodged a FIR, no further investigation could be carried out as no accused was arrested immediately. Vishnu Yakha (Rai) was arrested only 6 years after the incident. A chargesheet had been filed in the Morang District Court accusing four persons, including Vishnu Yakha (Rai) for committing intentional homicide. The district court found Bishnu Yakha Rai guilty and sentenced him to life imprisonment. The case had been adjourned as the other three defendants are absconding without appearing on the term of the warrant. Both the plaintiffs and the defendent appealed to the Appellate Court Biratnagar. The plaintiff (Government of Nepal ) seeks for the life imprisonment along with the confiscation of property while defendant Vishnu Yakha Rai seeks for the acquittal. Meanwhile, adjourned case had been decided by the district court to acquit other three defendants. Unsatisfied with this decision, the plaintiff (Government of Nepal) had appealed to the Appellate Court Biratnagar. The Appellate Court Biratnagar had heard three appeals together: two appeals from the plaintiff and one appeal from defendant (Bishnu Yakha Rai). One of the judges upheld the district court verdict that had sentenced Bishnu Yakha Rai to life imprisonment for conviction, while another judge ruled that reversed the district court's verdict and acquitted the defendant Vishnu Yakha Rai. Thus, after a disagreement between the judges of the division bench of the appellate court, it had been submitted to the third judge. The third judge upheld the opinion expressed by judge who acquitted Bishnu Kumar Yakha Rai. After failing to reach the appeal, the plaintiff government of Nepal appealed to the Supreme Court challenging the decision of the Appellate Court Biratnagar. The Supreme Court upheld the verdict of Appellate Court Biratnagar, the appeal of the plaintiff government of Nepal was not reached.

    सारसङ्क्षेपः कोभिड-१९ को महामारीको कारणले नेपाल सरकारबाट घोषणा भएको बन्दाबन्दी(Lockdown) को समयमा लैङ्गिक हिंसाबाट महिला तथा बालिकाहरू विशेष रूपमा प्रभावित भएका छन्। महिलाको प्रजजन स्वास्थ्यमासमेत गम्भीर असर परेको छ। सङ्घीय सरकारबाट गठित कोराना रोकथाम समितिमा महिलाको सहभागिता गराइएको छैन। कतिपय स्थानमा महिलाको निम्ति छुट्टै क्वारेन्टाइन छुट्याइएको पनि छैन। कोभिड सङ्क्रमणले मृत्यु भएका व्यक्तिहरूको विस्तृत विवरण सार्वजनिक गर्नाले गोपनियताको हकमा गम्भीर असर पुगेको छ। यी र यस्तै कारणहरूले गर्दा महिलाहरूको मौलिक हकमा विशेष रूपले आघात परेको हुँदा महिलाको अधिकारको प्रत्याभूत गर्न विपक्षीको नाउँमा परामदेश लगायतका उपयुक्त आदेश जारी गरी पाउँ भनी सर्वोच्च अदालतमा अधिवक्ता रोशनी पौड्यालद्वारा रिट दायर भयो।


    आदेश (फैसला)को सारः
    कोभिड -१९ को महामारीले महिलामा फरक र जटिल असर पुर्‍याएको र यसलाई Shadow Pandemic (छायाँ माहामारी) को रूपमा लिई सर्वोच्च अदालतबाट ती विषयमा feminist response भएको छ:


    • व्यक्तिका अत्यन्त निजी सूचनाहरू कुनै खास उद्देश्य र कानूनी प्रयोगजनको लागि बाहेक खुला गर्न नलगाउने

    • लैंङ्गिक हिंसाका घटनाको Online case reporting गर्ने व्यवस्था मिलाउने।

    • सङ्क्रामक रोग ऐन, २०२० लगायतका प्रचलित कानून प्रभावकारी छ, छैन अध्ययन गरी लैङ्गिक मैत्री र High Risk Group लाई उच्च प्राथमिकतामा राखी कानून बनाउन नेपाल सरकारको नाउँमा आदेश जारी हुेन

      • Supreme Court Order:
      Women and girls have been particularly affected by gender-based violence during the lockdown announced by the government of Nepal due to the Covid-19 pandemic. . The reproductive health of women has also been seriously affected. Women are not selected in the Corona Prevention Committee formed by the federal government. In many places, there is no separate quarantine for women. The publication of the details of people who have died due to the covid infection has seriously affected the right to privacy. Due to these and similar reasons, the fundamental rights of women have been specially affected, so a writ was filed by Advocate Roshni Paudyal in the Supreme Court asking that an appropriate order including Mandamus be issued in the name of the opponents to guarantee & secure the rights of women.


      Summary of the order (verdict):
      The epidemic of covid-19 has had different and complex effects on women and it has been a feminist response from the Supreme Court considering it as a Shadow Pandemic:


      • Not to disclose highly private information of individuals except for specific purposes and lawful uses

      • Arrange online case reporting of sexual violence incidents.

      • Whether infectious Diseases Act, 2020 and other existing laws are effective or not? An order was not issued in the name of the Government of Nepal to make a study on it & bring gender friendly and High Risk Group prioritised law.
